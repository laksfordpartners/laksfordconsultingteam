Questions

Good to know

Is there a contract or lock-in?

No lock-in. Plans are month-to-month — cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What counts as a "video edit"?

A short-form vertical edit (Reel / TikTok / Short) up to ~60 seconds — cut, captioned, and ready to post.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Move up or down at the start of any billing cycle, or bolt on add-ons without changing your plan.

Do you handle ad spend?

We manage the campaigns; your ad budget is paid directly to the platform and billed separately from our 15% management fee.

How do I get you my streams?

Just share your Twitch, YouTube, or Kick VODs — or give us channel access — and we pull the clips. No uploading or editing work on your side.

Which platforms do you clip for?

TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. On Growth and Always-On we format and post to all three.
