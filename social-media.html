Our framework

The Arvani305 Framework

The five-part structure behind short-form video that converts — refined with our client arvani305 and now taught to every team we work with as part of our social media management.

1 · Hook in 3 seconds

Open with the single most attention-grabbing moment so viewers stop scrolling before they ever decide to.

2 · Native, not polished

Content that feels like a real person, not an ad. Authenticity outperforms production value every time.

3 · Show the value

Demonstrate the product in use and the transformation it delivers — make the benefit impossible to miss.

4 · Social proof

Weave in reactions, reviews, and real customers so new viewers trust before they buy.

5 · One clear CTA

Tell viewers exactly what to do next. A single, obvious action turns interest into an add-to-cart.

Then: test & repeat

We teach your team to read the numbers, double down on winners, and keep cost-per-result falling.
