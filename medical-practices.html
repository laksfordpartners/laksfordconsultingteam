Success story · Dental

Growing a charitable dental clinic — on almost no budget

The Prajapati Group Charitable Foundation Dental Clinic in Panvel had no budget for a website or Google Ads. So we grew it the local-first way — with grassroots channels that reach real people where they already are.

Newspaper inserts

Local print inserts put the clinic in front of nearby households — and drove the most new patients of any channel.

JustDial listing

We listed the clinic on JustDial — India's go-to app for local businesses — so nearby patients could find and call it directly.

WhatsApp outreach

With zero ad spend, we promoted the clinic through targeted WhatsApp broadcasts to the local community.

Local influencers & video

We partnered with local influencers and posted patient-friendly videos on the clinic's own social channels.

What worked best: the humble newspaper insert outperformed everything — proof that the right channel for the audience beats the flashiest one. We test, measure, and pour budget into the winner.
